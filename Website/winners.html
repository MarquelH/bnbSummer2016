Kaitlin Hiciano

Kaitlin Hiciano graduated from Spring Valley High School, class of 2016 and attends Fordham University. Majoring in biology, Kaitlin aspires to become a forensic pathologist for a federal agency. Ellen Sue Cola

Ellen Sue Cola graduated from Ramapo Senior High School in the class of 2016. She attends Brown University where she studies Africana Studies and aspires to be a vocal leader in the current civil rights movement to bring about equality and justice to minorities, especially African Americans, in the educational, social, and political realms. Her dream is to work in the United States Department of Justice or in the White House Initiative on Educational Excellence for African Americans. Being of Haitian descent, Ellen's values have been shaped to always put others before her, reach out a helping hand, and be involved in community engagement. True success, described by Ellen, comes from within-- "Everyone has the chance to succeed; It is just on us to maximize that chance."

Favorite Quote:
"The noblest search is the search for excellence."
- President Lyndon B. Johnson
