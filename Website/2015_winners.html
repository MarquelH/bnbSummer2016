Brishelle Gamble

Brishelle Gamble graduated from Ramapo High School in the Spring of 2015. Currently, she is attending Northeastern University where she is majoring in Civil Engineering. She is most interested in studying the environmental branch this discipline, and is inspired to become apart of a community that works towards ecological solutions and extensive plans for sustainability. At school, Brishelle is an active member of a BESS, the black engineering student society, and the environmental action club. At these activities she is able to participate in local outreach programs, develop professional relationships, and impact the sustainability commitment that our school has made. Her ultimate goal in this field would be to become an asset that is able to inspire and assist those in our country who want improve the natural quality of their lives, the projected lives of future generations and the wellness of this Earth. She feels that she will be able to achieve this with the support snd guidance of her family, friends, fellow peers and mentors.
